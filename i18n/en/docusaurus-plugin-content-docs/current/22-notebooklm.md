# NotebookLM

[NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com/) is a Google tool that works as an AI-based personal research assistant.

## What makes it different?

Unlike ChatGPT or Gemini, NotebookLM is **"source-grounded"**: it only generates responses based on the documents you upload.

## Main features

### 📚 Supported fonts
- PDFs and Google Docs
- URLs of web pages
- YouTube videos (automatic transcription)
- Text files

### 🎧 Audio Overviews (Podcasts)
One of the most popular features: NotebookLM can generate a **conversational podcast** about your documents.

- Two "presenters" discuss the content naturally
- Customizable: duration, tone, focus
- Ideal for reviewing content "on the way" to class

### 🎬 Video Overviews
Similar to podcasts but in video format:
- Automatically generated visual presentations
- Available in more than 80 languages
- Customizable visual styles

### 📝 Study Guides and Quizzes
Automatically generate:
- Structured **Study Guides**
- **Test questions** based on content
- **Flashcards** for memorization
- **Condensed summaries**

### 🗺️ Mind Maps
Interactive mind maps for:
- Visualize relationships between concepts
- Explore connections in your research
- Synthesize information from multiple sources

## Educational use

### For students
- Prepare exams by generating quizzes from the notes
- Listen to podcast summaries while they study
- Organize information from multiple sources
- Get verifiable answers with quotes

### For teachers
- Create study materials quickly
- Generate test questions on the syllabus
- Prepare summaries for students
- Create educational podcasts without recording

## Comparison with ChatGPT Projects

| Feature | NotebookLM | ChatGPT Projects |
|----------------|-------------|------------------|
| Based on sources | ✅ Only your documents | ❌ Also use general knowledge |
| Generate podcasts | ✅ Audio Overviews | ❌ |
| Automatic appointments | ✅ With links | Partial |
| Free | ✅ | Only with Plus |
| Ideal for | Academic research | General projects |
